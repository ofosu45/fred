BENEFITS OF USING NHIS CARD

The National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) is a social intervention program
introduced by government to provide financial access to quality health care for residents in Ghana.
  • The NHIS covers outpatient services, including diagnostic testing and operations such as hernia repair
  • Most in-patient services, including specialist care, most surgeries, and hospital accommodation (general ward)
  • Oral health treatments; all maternity care services, including Caesarean deliveries; emergency care
